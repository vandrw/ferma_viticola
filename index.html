The Panciu vineyards

Located on the terraces of the curvature Carpathian foothills, Tifesti is at the heart of the winemaking region in the north of the Vrancea county, in the Panciu vineyards. Dealul Sârbilor is characterised by a bathicalcaric loamy clay soil developed on medium-fine loessoid deposits, by balanced humidity, moderate temperature (yearly average 10.2 ℃) and sun exposure on a slope of 3° E-SE.

Our work

In 2015 we entered the market with a tailor-made appearance, dedicated to the tastes of the modern consumer and to the quality of our wines. Thus, in order to make this possible, we worked intensively with specialized consulting teams to prepare a new strategy for the presence on the wine market. First of all, 3 basic aspects were targeted: CULTIVATION, PRODUCT and PLACEMENT.

Our grapes are our pride and we strive each year to create bountiful harvests. We believe that future generations should enjoy the flavour that we currently store.
